Employee Promotion Prediction using Machine Learning involves leveraging historical employee data to forecast whether an employee is likely to be promoted. This process begins with data collection, encompassing employee demographics, job-related information, performance metrics, training records, and past promotion history. The data is then preprocessed through cleaning, handling missing values, and feature engineering to enhance the dataset's quality. Machine learning algorithms, such as decision trees, random forests, or logistic regression, are trained on this preprocessed data to identify patterns and relationships between employee characteristics and promotion outcomes. The model can then be used to predict promotion likelihood, aiding organizations in making data-driven promotion decisions, optimizing talent management, and enhancing workforce planning.
